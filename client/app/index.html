About Nerds vs. Jocks

One day I was watching ESPN and thinking, "Wow, the half my buddies have better analysis than these guys. Then it hit me, why don't we start a site where fans can deilver their opions and actually back them.

Between fantasy analysis, game predictions, and in-depth articles, we are giving fans the ability to prove their stuff.

Eventually, I want this to become a marketplace where fans who know their stuff and deliver insight can actually get paid for it. Sort of like a side hustle or passive income. This is going to be an experiment so bear with me through this journey. And PLEASE PLEASE PLEASE provide feedback.

The GamePlan

Overall the Goal is to identify the fans who provide the best insight and information. The way we will do this is by giving every user a credibility score. This credibility score will be determined based on predictions made in the fantasy realm and gamecenter, analysis provided in the team forums, articles written and feedback from users on those articles. In addition, there may be some other experiments but we'll figure that out along the way.
